Author's note

Alien Encounters font by ShyWedge is a free modern display typeface that looks like it’s been beamed straight off a vintage sci‑fi monitor. Its tall, squared letters are sliced by tight horizontal bands, creating a slick scanline effect that feels both retro and futuristic. The rhythm of the gaps gives every word a subtle flicker, like it’s vibrating on a starship console or glitching on an old CRT screen.

Using Alien Encounters, bold titles instantly gain a techno, otherworldly presence that doesn’t need extra effects to stand out. This free font shines on sci‑fi posters, gaming logos, synthwave or EDM cover art, streaming overlays, tech event branding, and movie title cards. Keep it big and airy with plenty of letter spacing, and pair it with a clean geometric sans for body copy to let those striped letterforms really hum.
